# Code of conduct

## Our standard

BridgeBench welcomes builders who contribute code, tasks, audits, and criticism in good faith.

Expected behavior:

- challenge evidence and methodology without attacking people;
- explain claims with reproducible examples;
- respect confidentiality around active hidden references and security reports;
- disclose conflicts of interest when evaluating models or vendors;
- accept correction when the record contradicts a claim.

Unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, threats, discrimination, or personal attacks;
- publishing private information without permission;
- deliberately leaking active hidden references;
- manipulating benchmark artifacts or results;
- sustained disruption after a maintainer asks for a change in behavior.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately through the repository's security-reporting channel. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions, close threads, restrict participation, or ban accounts when needed to protect the project and its contributors.

Enforcement decisions should be proportionate, documented privately, and free from retaliation.
